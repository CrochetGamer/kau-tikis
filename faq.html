Frequently Asked Questions

We certainly get A TON of questions about our business and processes. People want to know everything from shipping costs to how we make our tikis. We get really good, questions....and at times, we get really dumb questions. We answer them all here! (This is a long section, so bring popcorn along. Seriously.)

Questions about Our Products

QUESTION: Can I get a refund?

ANSWER: No. We do not offer refunds. After an order is placed and we deliver the item safely to the customer, it is up to the customer to care for the item.

QUESTION: Yeah, but I really need a refund because I didn't know this sculpture was this tall/short.

ANSWER: You should have paid attention to the information warning label located on each sculpture that explains the sculpture dimensions in inches for height and width. The warning even explains that you should have a measuring tape or ruler on hand so you can understand how tall/short each sculpture is. We do not give refunds because you didn't read the warning section.

QUESTION: Can you make me a custom tiki, planter, or sculpture?

ANSWER: Yes and no. At times, we may gladly accept custom requests under certain circumstances. It depends on many aspects of the custom job. Obviously, a custom order will cost more money and will be subject to a custom materials charge. At times, we may decline certain custom orders due to lack of time, availability of materials, or operating cost. Usually, the customer declines after they realize how expensive a custom concrete sculpture can be.

QUESTION: I like one of your sculptures but I want you to change something on it. Can you do that?

ANSWER: No, we cannot change an already created concrete sculpture to suit your needs.

QUESTION: Can you make me the same tiki but make it 4, 5, 6, or even 7 foot tall?

ANSWER: No. We do not offer larger or smaller sizes of each tiki. Each tiki is designed to be a specific size, and we cannot randomly make gigantic versions.

QUESTION: I'd like to order a custom tiki that's 4 ft tall or bigger, with the same price and shipping as the cheap plastic/resin tiki I saw on Amazon/Wayfair/Temu/Etsy. Is that possible?

ANSWER: Yikes! You should really think logically about this question before you ask it. You are requesting custom work to go into a huge concrete sculpture, which means that the price will obviously be higher than just purchasing a cheap product online.

For a custom job, we charge time and materials going into that agreement. We require all custom orders to be signed by the customer before we start. That means that you have to pay for part of that custom work before we start it because we have to purchase new materials and take separate time apart from our regular deliveries and business hours to create your custom sculpture.

So no, just because you send us a picture of a big, cheaply made, resin sculpture you found online and explain that you are expecting to pay less than the online price for a custom, handmade concrete sculpture, doesn't mean we are going to agree and start your sculpture.

QUESTION: Can I make anything custom?

ANSWER: This depends on the order. First, we speak with the customer. It's possible they want something that we just can't make or don't want to make. (Someone once asked us to make them a concrete statue of a college football mascot holding the name of their son - copyright issues and our hatred for sports stopped that custom order in it's tracks!)

QUESTION: What are the dimensions of this sculpture?

ANSWER: Each statue and item on our website has information attached in the product specification section that explains width, height, and thickness in inches. We recommend referencing a measuring tape or ruler if you need to see how tall or wide each sculpture is.

QUESTION: What is the spiritual meaning behind your tikis?

ANSWER: Absolutely nothing. Our tikis are literally concrete garden art statues. If you think that concrete has a spiritual meaning, then you might as well worship the sidewalk in front of your house. We are Christian, and believe that Jesus Christ is God. He alone is worthy of your worship, not a concrete statue that we designed for fun.

QUESTION: Hey...wait a minute...you're not Hawaiian! Isn't making and selling tikis considered cultural misappropriation?

ANSWER: Are you going to tell me that you have to be Italian to own a pizzaria? Or how about this: next time you crave Chinese food, you should slap yourself because you aren't Chinese. Yeah right. And yes - people really do say dumb things like this to us.

QUESTION: Can you explain the price differences on bare and painted sculptures?

ANSWER: Sure! When you purchase a bare concrete sculpture from us, you are receiving a non-painted and non-sealed sculpture. This is the base sculpture, so it takes less time to make.

When you purchase a painted sculpture from us, you are receiving a painted and weatherproof-sealed sculpture, which takes us much more time to make. That's why we typically charge between $5 and $30 extra per sculpture. (Painting and sealing price varies depending on sculpture.)

For example, our 13 inch tall Starfish Tiki costs $40 as a bare sculpture with no sealant or painting. For the painted version of the same tiki, the price goes up to $60. We added $20 onto the base price to cover the cost of time and materials needed to paint the tiki and seal it. We offer both versions for sale. Or, you could purchase a bare tiki and paint it yourself!

Questions about Our Business & Deliveries

QUESTION: Do you do farmers markets?

ANSWER: No. We do not attend farmers markets on the Big Island at this time.

QUESTION: Where are you located? Do you have a store or shop?

ANSWER: We are located in Ocean View on the Big Island of Hawaii and only operate through placed orders & deliveries - this means we do not have a storefront at this time.

QUESTION: Can you deliver to my house?

ANSWER: No. We do not offer delivery to customer homes. Instead, we meet up with clients in safe public places. This protects us & the customers.

QUESTION: Will you wholesale sculptures to my shop/business?

ANSWER: No. We cannot take wholesale orders at this time.

QUESTION: What days should I expect delivery to take place?

ANSWER: We normally deliver on weekdays (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday) and we avoid deliveries on the weekends unless every single customer has to meet us on Saturday. This is due to traffic. Saturday & Sundays are too busy traveling to and from Hilo & Kona and navigating in the cities.

QUESTION: How much does delivery cost? To what areas do you deliver?

ANSWER: Delivery in Ocean View and to Na'alehu is free, but delivery to Pahala, Hilo, Kealakekua, and Kona requires a $15 fee per order. We cannot deliver to Waimea due to distance. If you live in Waimea and would like to order, please try to meet up with us in Hilo or Kona on the days we deliver there.

QUESTION: Can you waive my delivery fee? Is it really necessary?

ANSWER: No we cannot waive it. Yes it is necessary. Driving to Hilo or Kona is a 4 hour round trip for us. We are able to make deliveries work by charging each customer $15, which covers our gas cost. This allows us to continue making deliveries.

QUESTION: Can I get a discount on bulk quantities?

ANSWER: No. We do not offer discounts on bulk quantities as each piece takes several days to make.

QUESTION: Can't you cut me some kind of deal?

ANSWER: No. All customers are charged the same price. We do run sales every now and then, so be on the look out and sign up for our newsletter for coupon codes.
